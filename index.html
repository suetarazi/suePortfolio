About Me

I'm Dr. Sue Tarazi, and I believe in giving people a better quality of life. After Owning a Physical Therapy Private Practice for over 9 years that operated in the black every year, I decided to branch out and make the transition and become a software developer.
I still love health and wellness as well as caring for others on a large scale, but being in tech actually allows me to work with a team on a product or service that can improve the lives of a greater number of people.
I bring with me the skills of organizing and running a project, excellent problem solving skills, the ability to connect with others as well as my lifelong love of learning.
